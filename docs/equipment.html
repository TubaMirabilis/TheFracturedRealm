Equipment

In The Fractured Realm, players have access to a wide range of equipment to outfit their armies and heroes. From basic weapons and armor to powerful artifacts and magical items, players can customize their units and characters to suit their playstyle and strategy.

Types of Equipment

  • Weapons:

    Swords, axes, bows, and other weapons provide different bonuses and abilities to units in combat.

  • Armor:

    Armor pieces like helmets, chestplates, and shields can increase a unit's defense and survivability in battle.

  • Artifacts:

    Powerful relics with unique effects that can turn the tide of battle in favor of the player.

  • Consumables:

    Potions, scrolls, and other consumable items that provide temporary bonuses or effects to units.

  • Magical Items:

    Enchanted items that grant units special abilities or powers beyond their normal capabilities.

Acquiring Equipment

Players can acquire equipment through various means, including:

  • Exploration:

    Discovering hidden caches, treasure troves, and ancient ruins can yield valuable equipment.

  • Quests:

    Completing quests and missions can reward players with powerful weapons and armor.

  • Crafting:

    Crafting items using resources gathered from the game world allows players to create custom equipment.

  • Merchants:

    Visiting merchants and traders in towns and cities provides access to a variety of equipment for sale.

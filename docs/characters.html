Characters

Player Characters

In The Fractured Realm, players can create and customize their own characters to represent themselves in the game world. Each character has unique abilities, strengths, and weaknesses that can be developed over time.

Non-Player Characters

The game world is populated by a variety of non-player characters, including nobles, generals, merchants, and other figures. These characters have their own goals, motivations, and relationships with the player and other NPCs.

Companions

Players can recruit companions to join them on their adventures. These companions have their own abilities and personalities, and can provide valuable support in combat and other situations.

Enemies

The game world is also home to a variety of enemies, including rival factions, beasts, and other threats. Players must overcome these challenges to achieve their goals and establish their dominion.

Character Progression

Characters can gain experience, level up, and acquire new skills and abilities as they progress through the game. Players can customize their characters' development to suit their playing style and strategic goals.

Character Creation

Players can create characters by choosing from a variety of races, classes, and backgrounds. Each choice affects the character's abilities, strengths, and weaknesses, as well as their starting equipment and resources.

Follow these steps to create a character:

  1. Use your MUD client to connect to the game server.
  2. Enter the character creation command create-character.
  3. Follow the prompts to customize your character's attributes and skills.

Races

Eldrii

Eldrii are ancient beings with elongated lifespans, known for their wisdom and arcane abilities. Their deep understanding of magic makes them excellent strategists and feared in diplomatic circles. Eldrii are often found in positions of power and influence, guiding the fate of nations. They are known for their tall, slender frames, pointed ears, and luminous eyes. Eldrii have a natural affinity for magic and can excel in spellcasting classes. They are also skilled in diplomacy and negotiation, making them ideal leaders and advisors. Eldrii characters receive bonuses to intelligence, charisma, and magical abilities.

Tarkens

Hardy and resilient, these human descendants have adapted to the rugged terrains of the fractured empire. Tarkens are exceptional at resource management, able to glean more from the land than others. They are known for their stocky builds, weathered features, and practical outlook on life. Tarkens excel in survival skills, crafting, and combat, making them ideal for physical classes. They are also known for their loyalty and sense of duty, often serving as soldiers, guards, and laborers. Tarken characters receive bonuses to strength, constitution, and resource management skills.

Vaelora

Vaelora are elf-like creatures with a close connection to nature, making them adept at guerilla warfare and espionage. Their knowledge of the natural world grants them bonuses in foraging and tracking skills. Vaelora are known for their lithe frames, pointed features, and keen senses. They excel in archery, stealth, and survival, making them ideal for ranger and rogue classes. Vaelora characters receive bonuses to dexterity, perception, and nature-related skills.

Synthkin

Originally created as servants, these biomechanical beings have gained independence. They are highly adaptable and can be customized with various mechanical enhancements for specific tasks or combat roles. Synthkin are known for their metallic bodies, glowing eyes, and modular limbs. They excel in engineering, technology, and combat, making them ideal for artificer and warrior classes. Synthkin characters receive bonuses to intelligence, strength, and mechanical skills.

Umbrae

Umbrae are shadowy figures that excel in stealth and subterfuge. Their mysterious origins and abilities to blend into darkness make them perfect for missions involving theft, espionage, or assassination. Umbrae are known for their dark, ethereal forms, glowing eyes, and silent movements. They excel in stealth, deception, and combat, making them ideal for rogue and assassin classes. Umbrae characters receive bonuses to dexterity, perception, and stealth skills.

Classes

Warrior

Warriors are masters of combat, skilled in a variety of weapons and fighting styles. They excel in melee combat, able to withstand and deliver powerful blows. Warriors are known for their strength, endurance, and martial prowess. They can specialize in different combat styles, such as two-handed weapons, sword and shield, or dual-wielding. Warriors are often found leading armies, serving as bodyguards, or competing in tournaments. Warrior characters receive bonuses to strength, constitution, and combat skills.

Ranger

Rangers are skilled hunters and trackers, adept at surviving in the wilderness and navigating treacherous terrains. They excel in archery, stealth, and nature-related skills. Rangers are known for their marksmanship, resourcefulness, and connection to the natural world. They can specialize in different types of bows, traps, and animal companions. Rangers are often found patrolling borders, exploring uncharted territories, or hunting dangerous beasts. Ranger characters receive bonuses to dexterity, perception, and survival skills.

Rogue

Rogues are masters of stealth and deception, skilled in infiltration, sabotage, and subterfuge. They excel in lockpicking, pickpocketing, and assassination. Rogues are known for their agility, cunning, and resourcefulness. They can specialize in different rogue archetypes, such as thieves, assassins, or spies. Rogues are often found working as spies, thieves' guild members, or assassins for hire. Rogue characters receive bonuses to dexterity, perception, and stealth skills.

Artificer

Artificers are masters of technology and engineering, skilled in crafting, enchanting, and creating magical devices. They excel in inventing gadgets, repairing machinery, and imbuing items with magical properties. Artificers are known for their intelligence, creativity, and ingenuity. They can specialize in different types of devices, such as firearms, golems, or alchemical potions. Artificers are often found working as inventors, tinkerers, or magical engineers. Artificer characters receive bonuses to intelligence, mechanical skills, and magical abilities.

Spellcaster

Spellcasters are masters of magic, skilled in casting spells, summoning creatures, and manipulating the elements. They excel in spellcasting, enchanting, and divination. Spellcasters are known for their knowledge of arcane and divine magic, their ability to shape reality with their will, and their connection to otherworldly forces. They can specialize in different schools of magic, such as elemental magic, necromancy, or illusion. Spellcasters are often found serving as court mages, temple priests, or wandering wizards. Spellcaster characters receive bonuses to intelligence, magical abilities, and spellcasting skills.

Character Profiles

Players can view their character profiles to track their progress, achievements, and relationships with other characters. The profile displays information such as the character's level, experience points, equipment, and faction affiliations.

Type profile in the game to view your character profile.

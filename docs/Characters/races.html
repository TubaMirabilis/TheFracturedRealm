Races

Eldrii

Eldrii are ancient, long-lived beings recognized for their wisdom, strategic acumen, and arcane prowess. Known for tall, slender frames, pointed ears, and luminous eyes, they have a natural affinity for magic and diplomacy. Legends say Eldrii were the first to harness magic, shaping the world and unlocking ancient secrets. They excel in spellcasting classes, statesmanship, and research, making them influential leaders, advisors, and scholars.

Tarkens

Tarkens are hardy descendants of humans who have adapted to harsh terrains. Stocky and resilient, they are noted for strength, endurance, and practical skills. They excel at survival, crafting, and combat, often serving as soldiers, guards, or laborers. Tarkens thrive in rugged environments, relying on resourcefulness, courage, and duty to endure and protect their communities.

Vaelora

Vaelora are elf-like beings closely attuned to nature, excelling in archery, stealth, and survival. With keen senses and lithe forms, they make formidable hunters, rangers, and scouts. Living harmoniously with the wilds, Vaelora master the bow, thrive in remote forests and mountains, and rely on cunning and agility to overcome challenges.

Umbrae

Umbrae are shadowy figures skilled in stealth, subterfuge, and infiltration. Their dark, ethereal appearance and glowing eyes hint at mysterious origins. They excel as rogues and assassins, blending into darkness to strike unseen. Their silence, cunning, and adaptability make them invaluable in covert operations and espionage.

Characters

Player Characters

In The Fractured Realm, players create and customize their own characters. Each character has unique abilities, strengths, and weaknesses that can be developed over time, reflecting the player's chosen playstyle.

Non-Player Characters

The world is populated by various non-player characters (NPCs), including nobles, generals, merchants, and other figures. These NPCs have their own goals, motivations, and relationships, influencing the player's experiences and the unfolding narrative.

Companions

Players can recruit companions to join their adventures. These allies have distinctive abilities, personalities, and provide valuable support in combat, exploration, and other situations.

Enemies

A variety of enemies—rival factions, beasts, and other threats—populate the realm. Overcoming these adversaries is essential to achieving goals, securing resources, and establishing dominion.

Character Progression

As characters gain experience, they can level up, acquire new skills, and enhance their abilities. Players customize their character's development to suit personal strategies and preferences.

Character Creation

New characters are shaped by choices of race, class, and background. Each selection affects abilities, starting equipment, resources, and initial opportunities.

Follow these steps to create a character:

  1. Use your MUD client to connect to the game server.
  2. Enter the command create-character.
  3. Follow the prompts to set your character's attributes and skills.

Character Profiles

Character profiles display progress, achievements, equipment, and affiliations. They help players track development and plan future goals.

Type profile in the game to view your character profile.

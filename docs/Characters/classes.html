Classes

Warrior

Warriors are combat experts trained in various weapons and fighting styles. Strong, enduring, and disciplined, they thrive in melee engagements and specialize in tactics like wielding heavy weapons or combining sword and shield. Warriors often serve as soldiers, captains, or bodyguards, and love testing their prowess in competitive arenas.

Ranger

Rangers are skilled hunters and trackers, adept at archery, stealth, and surviving hostile environments. They excel at navigating treacherous terrains and often patrol borders, explore uncharted lands, or hunt dangerous beasts.

Rogue

Rogues master stealth and deception, excelling in infiltration, sabotage, and silent eliminations. Agile and cunning, they can pick locks, steal valuables, and vanish without trace. Rogues commonly serve as thieves, assassins, or spies.

Artificer

Artificers are inventive engineers who blend technology and magic to create wondrous devices. Known for intelligence, creativity, and ingenuity, they specialize in crafting enchanted gear, repairing machinery, and designing gadgets that grant powerful advantages.

Spellcaster

Spellcasters wield arcane and divine magic to reshape reality, summon creatures, and manipulate the elements. They can focus on elemental magic, necromancy, illusion, or other schools. Spellcasters often serve as court mages, temple priests, or wandering scholars of the arcane.

Backgrounds

Noble

Nobles are born into wealth, education, and influence. Skilled in diplomacy, leadership, and etiquette, they navigate complex hierarchies with ease, often managing estates, hosting grand events, or negotiating treaties. Their refined manners and high status grant them access to privileges others can only imagine.

Outlaw

Outlaws live beyond the law, thriving on stealth, deception, and survival. They reject authority, forging their own paths as bandits, pirates, or freedom fighters. Renowned for rugged independence, they raid caravans, challenge oppressive regimes, and rely on cunning to endure in hostile worlds.

Merchant

Merchants are savvy traders and resource managers who excel in negotiation and commerce. They profit from buying, selling, and investing, often rising to positions of wealth and influence. Whether guildmasters or smugglers, merchants shape markets, finance expeditions, and exploit every opportunity for gain.

Peasant

Peasants are humble folk working the land, skilled in farming, animal husbandry, and survival. They know the value of hard work, stewardship, and resilience. Often tending fields or raising livestock, peasants form the foundation of agrarian life and rural communities.

Academic

Academics are scholars, researchers, and teachers dedicated to knowledge, learning, and the arcane. They study ancient texts, conduct experiments, and impart wisdom. Whether historians, alchemists, or theologians, academics uncover secrets, push boundaries, and shape intellectual life in libraries, laboratories, and temples.

Adventurer

Adventurers seek fame, fortune, and glory. Skilled in survival, combat, and treasure hunting, they brave ruins and dungeons in pursuit of wealth and renown. Whether explorers, monster slayers, or treasure hunters, adventurers find excitement and risk around every corner.

Entertainer

Entertainers are performers who captivate audiences with music, dance, storytelling, and other arts. Using creativity, charisma, and passion, they bring joy and unity to taverns, theaters, and courts. As bards, jesters, or actors, entertainers enliven a troubled world with moments of wonder.

Hermit

Hermits live in isolation, seeking wisdom, spiritual insight, and communion with the divine. Skilled in meditation and divination, they often serve as healers, prophets, or ascetics, guiding seekers toward enlightenment. They dwell in remote places, contemplating mysteries and offering solace to those who find them.

Scoundrel

Scoundrels are charming tricksters who thrive on deception, gambling, and mischief. They relish chaos, find clever ways to profit, and delight in outwitting foes. As con artists, gamblers, or pranksters, scoundrels amuse, baffle, and confound the unwary.

Wanderer

Wanderers are nomads and travelers who cherish freedom, exploration, and discovery. Skilled in navigation, survival, and storytelling, they follow the open road to distant horizons. As explorers, traders, or guides, wanderers rely on independence, curiosity, and adaptability to thrive.

Zealot

Zealots are true believers, devoted to a cause, deity, or ideology. Skilled in persuasion, devotion, and combat, they inspire others to join their crusade. Holy warriors, cult leaders, or political agitators, zealots fight fervently to reshape the world according to their convictions.
